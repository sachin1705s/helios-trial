## Viability Assessment

### ✅ This is viable. Here's why:
1. **Most posts are event-driven** — you're recapping things that already happened, not creating from scratch
2. **Notes-style posts are fast** — "3 things I learned from X" writes itself once you have bullet points
3. **The cadence tapers naturally** — Week 2 is lighter than Week 1, giving you breathing room

### ⚠️ Watch out for:
1. **Double-post days (7 of them)** — These only work if the evening post is short (3-5 lines max). Don't write two long-form posts in one day.
2. **Tuesday May 6 is your only single-post day in Week 1** — Consider banking a short post for that evening as a buffer
3. **Repurposing for X** — LinkedIn posts need to be shortened for X. Rule of thumb: take the hook + one key insight + CTA. Don't copy-paste.

### 💡 Suggested workflow:
1. You write rough drafts with bullet points / voice notes
2. I shape them into LinkedIn posts + X repurposed versions
3. We mark them ✅ as they're finalized
